## Overview

Upload your CV or paste your LinkedIn URL and get a complete, deployed portfolio site in 60 seconds. GPT-4 writes the copy; the tool deploys to Vercel automatically.

## Features

- **CV parsing** — extracts skills, experience, and projects from PDF
- **AI copywriting** — generates professional bio, project descriptions, skills summary
- **Template selection** — 5 portfolio templates to choose from
- **One-click deploy** — deploys to Vercel with custom subdomain
- **Edit after generation** — modify any section before publishing

## Results

- 50+ portfolios deployed
- Average setup time: 90 seconds
