## Overview

A production-ready e-commerce platform with AI-powered personalization. Built for a Kazakhstani clothing brand processing 50+ orders monthly.

## Features

- **AI recommendations** — GPT-4 embeddings for "you may also like"
- **Smart search** — semantic search understands natural language queries
- **Stripe payments** — secure checkout with card and KaspiPay
- **Admin dashboard** — order management, inventory, analytics
- **Personalized homepage** — adapts based on browsing history

## Results

- 50+ monthly orders
- 25% increase in average order value from recommendations
- 40% of sales come from recommendation clicks
