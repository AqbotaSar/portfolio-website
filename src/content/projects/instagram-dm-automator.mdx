## Overview

An automated Instagram DM system that turns followers into leads using n8n workflows and GPT-4. Built for a beauty salon that was spending 3 hours daily on DM responses.

## Features

- **Auto-response** to DMs with personalized AI-generated messages
- **Lead qualification** — identifies high-intent messages and flags them
- **Story mention replies** — automatically responds to story mentions
- **CRM sync** — qualified leads pushed to Airtable with full context
- **Follow-up sequences** — automated follow-up messages for cold leads

## Results

- 3 hours/day saved on manual DM responses
- 40% increase in lead conversion rate
- 150+ leads captured monthly
