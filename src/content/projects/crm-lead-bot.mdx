## Overview

A Telegram bot that acts as a first-touch sales assistant. Qualifies leads through a natural conversation, assigns a lead score, and pushes qualified contacts to CRM automatically.

## Features

- **Conversational qualification** — asks budget, timeline, and requirement questions naturally
- **Lead scoring** — assigns 0-100 score based on responses
- **Multi-CRM sync** — supports Airtable, Notion, and Bitrix24
- **Instant notifications** — alerts sales team via Telegram when hot lead detected
- **Conversation transcripts** — full chat logs saved with each lead record

## Results

- Used by 3 agencies
- 45% reduction in unqualified discovery calls
- 2x improvement in sales team efficiency
