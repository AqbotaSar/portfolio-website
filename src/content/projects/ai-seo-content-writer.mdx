## Overview

A content marketing tool that combines GPT-4 with SEO data to produce articles that rank. Integrates keyword research, SERP analysis, and content generation in one workflow.

## Features

- **Keyword research** — pulls search volume and competition data via SerpAPI
- **Competitor analysis** — analyzes top-10 ranking articles for a keyword
- **Long-form generation** — produces 1,500-3,000 word articles with proper structure
- **Meta tag generator** — title, description, OG tags auto-generated
- **Export** — download as HTML, Markdown, or WordPress XML

## Results

- 300+ articles generated for clients
- 70% of generated articles rank on page 1 within 90 days
