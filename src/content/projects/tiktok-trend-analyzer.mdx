## Overview

A Python-based tool that scrapes TikTok trending videos in a given niche, analyzes patterns with GPT-4, and generates content recommendations.

## Features

- **Trend scraping** — collects top-performing videos by hashtag/niche
- **Pattern analysis** — identifies common hooks, durations, and formats
- **Content suggestions** — generates 10 video ideas based on trends
- **Posting schedule** — recommends optimal posting times by audience timezone
- **Competitor tracking** — monitors specified accounts for strategy insights

## Results

- Used by 5 content creators
- Average 35% increase in video reach within 30 days
